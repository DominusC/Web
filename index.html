School facts and information

School purpose

Jack School is focused on creating educational opportunities for people from many lived experiences.

School culture

A culture where everyone can thrive. We are focused on creating a vibrant community of belonging that pursues diversity, builds inclusion, and creates more equitable opportunities for learning.

School course

Academic programs for a variety of learners The Jack School offers 6 courses in computing that provide teaching and imparting skills to students aiming to become computer scientists and engineers. We also offer countless non-degree opportunities for professional and lifelong learners, including executive education, continuing education and online courses.

School motto

The motto of Columbia University is "In thy light shall we see light." It can be translated into Chinese as "By your light, we can see the light" or "In the spirit of God we seek knowledge", which is based on the "Old Testament Psalms": "Quoniam apud te fons vitae in lumine tuo videbimus lumen" (Latin). The English translation is: "For with there is the fountain of life; and in the light we shall see light."The school motto has a strong religious flavor, but it does not hinder people's determination and courage to pursue truth and find light.

Career opportunities

We cooperate with the world's top Internet companies to ensure that our graduates can successfully go to the world's leading Internet companies for internships and jobs, such as Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Facebook, etc.

School philosophy

The establishment of Jack School coincides with the industrial revolution and the reform of higher education, which makes its school-running ideas all engraved with the brand of the times, and all show the spirit of pragmatism and entrepreneurship. At the beginning of its establishment, Jack School established a clear school purpose, that is, "to make what students learn directly useful to students' lives and help them succeed. Therefore, its purpose is to serve the ultimate benefit of the civilization and progress of the whole human being, Actively play the role of the University to promote the well-being of society; to teach students to obey the law and to enjoy the joys of freedom; to educate students to respect and love the noble principles embedded in democracy – because these noble principles are derived from people’s lives, liberties and the inalienable right of the pursuit of happiness".
